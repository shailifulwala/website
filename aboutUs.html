lets know about nail art

Nail Art is a creative form of painting, designing, decorating, and embellishing one's own nails. It is one of the unique art forms that displays a form of personal body art. It is an activity that allows you to draw picture designs on fingernails. Nail Art encompasses the basic techniques of decorative art in order to beautify fingernails as well as toenails. It enhances the beauty of both fingernails; toenails. The practice of painting fingernails is a fun way to brighten up your everyday look or accessorize a special occasion outfit. The surface of the nails can be ornamented by applying basic tips techniques of decorative artwork on fingernails. The artwork for nails comprehends the diverse overlay of Nail polish or UV Gels and/or Hybrid coating like Shellac. Colors, Glitters, Textures; Patterns are utilized in a wide variety for embellishing the nail surface. History Although Nail Art is considered modern nowadays, in reality, it dates back to early civilization.
